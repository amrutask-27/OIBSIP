About Us

Since 2010, Café Bliss has been serving handcrafted coffee and freshly baked pastries. We believe in cozy spaces, warm smiles, and unforgettable flavors.

Our beans are sourced ethically from local farmers and roasted to perfection to bring out the unique flavors in every cup. Along with our signature coffees, we offer a wide selection of teas, fresh juices, and artisan desserts.

Whether you're here for a quick morning coffee, an afternoon study session, or a relaxed evening with friends, Café Bliss is your perfect spot to unwind. Step in, and let the aroma of freshly brewed coffee make you feel at home.
